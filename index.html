About Coding Club of IITG

The Online Coding Club (ODP) at IIT Guwahati stands as a dynamic platform tailored for students enrolled in the Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence program, offered through the Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (MFDSAI). This initiative is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, fostering a community where learners can collaborate, innovate, and enhance their coding skills in real-world scenarios. Through a series of workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects, the ODP provides an environment that encourages continuous learning and adaptation to the rapidly evolving tech landscape. By engaging with the ODP, students not only refine their technical competencies but also develop critical soft skills such as teamwork, problem-solving, and effective communication, all of which are essential for success in the fields of data science and artificial intelligence.
